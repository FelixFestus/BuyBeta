OUR HISTORY

BuyBeta Supermarket was incorporated in 2020 with operations flagging off 23rd February, same year. The idea emanated from a strong desire to build a wholly indigenous retail chain. Within five years from inception from a single branch, BuyBeta Supermarket has grown into four branches, well run and positioned to become one of Africa's fastest growing brands in the retail sector. The BuyBeta Supermarket anywhere it is found, is a hub where shoppers needs are met in a one stop location.

Satisfactory Customer Service

Since we oppened our first branch on the 23rd of february 2020, we've been dedicated to making a difference in the lives of our customers. Our business is the result of Mr John Okeke's visionary leadership, along with our exceptional staff focused on helping customers and communities save money and live better. This values defines who we are and what we do today.


Our mission in business is to show that we can serve the public with honesty and integrity and in such a way where everyone wins. We always bear in mind that satisfactory customer service is one of our core values while we empower our staff and suppliers to contribute to the environment where we operate and the society at large.

Our Vision Statement
