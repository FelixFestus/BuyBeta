Changing the face of retail in West Africa




At BuyBeta, we have a deep understanding, that our work isn't just about what we achieve but how we achieve it. The way we conduct our business is as important as the products we sell and the services we provide. That means complying with the laws of the communities where we do business. But that is not enough. Staff who work at Supersaver supermarket are expected to operate based on our high standards and values—respect, service, excellence, and integrity.

Our mission in business is to show that we can serve the public with honesty and integrity and in such a way where everyone wins.
